Ingredients

    2 cups grated carrot
    1 tsp vanilla essence
    1/2 cup white sugar
    1/2 cup brown sugar
    14 cup milk
    2/3 cup coconut oil
    3/4 cup whole wheat flour
    1/2 tsp baking powder
    1/2 tsp baking powder
    1/4 tsp salt
    1/2 tsp cinnamon

Method

  1. Mix the carrot, vanilla, white and brown sugar together very well. Then add the milk and coconut oil and stir well with a spoon.
  2. Whisk together all the dry ingredients in a separate bowl until thoroughly combined. Add it to the wet ingredient bowl.
  3. Your batter is ready when both mixes are thoroughly combined. Preheat the oven at 180 degrees Celcius for 15 minutes.
  4. Grease and line a caketin before greasing it again. Fill it with the batter and bake for 18-25 minutes at 180 degrees Celcius.
  5. Enjoy!
